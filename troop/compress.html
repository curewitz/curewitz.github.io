How to Compress a Folder

(To make pictures larger just clikc on the image and then use the back button to return to this page)

Step 1: Right click on the folder you want to compress (.zip)

Step 2: Next hover the mouse down to Send To

Step 3: Next left click on Compressed (zipped) folder

This should send all the images in the folder you selected to a compressed folder

Step 4: Next left click anywhere in Windows Explorer to deselect the rename option

Once you have completed these steps you are all set to upload the compressed folder
To upload folder just browse to the location you sent the Compressed (zipped) folder to and click the open button
